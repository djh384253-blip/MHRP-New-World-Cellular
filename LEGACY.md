In Memory of Mandy Hebert
October 25, 2023

MHRP exists because of a life that deserved far more love and stability than this world provided. This project is a testament to the belief that even from a place of immense pain and regret, one can still create something beautiful for others.

Mandy Hebert was a person who believed in the potential of others—especially when they couldn't see it themselves. She held a light for those she loved, and it is that light we intend to carry forward through this work.

## The Purpose of This Name

This project is named MHRP to ensure that her name is associated with service, empathy, and the infrastructure of a better world. We are building this not as a monument to tragedy, but as a living, functional network that serves humanity.

## A Stark Reminder

We build this system with a heavy awareness of how quickly everything can change. This project serves as a permanent, functional reminder: never let the heat of a moment dictate the finality of your story. There is always a path to redemption, always a way to contribute to something greater.

To my mother: This is for you. May your name be a source of safety and connection for all who use this network.
