In Memory of Mandy Hebert
October 25, 2023
MHRP exists because of a life that deserved far more love and stability than this world provided. This project is a testament to the belief that even from a place of immense pain and regret, one can commit to building something that helps others.  
Mandy Hebert was a person who believed in the potential of others—especially when they couldn't see it themselves. She held a light for those she loved, and it is that light we intend to carry forward through this network.  
The Purpose of This Name
This project is named MHRP to ensure that her name is associated with service, empathy, and the infrastructure of a better world. We are building this not as a monument to tragedy, but as a living legacy. Every node deployed, every person helped, and every secure connection made is a way to pour all the love she deserves into a world that often feels indifferent.  
A Stark Reminder
We build this system with a heavy awareness of how quickly everything can change. This project serves as a permanent, functional reminder: never let the heat of a moment dictate the finality of your words. No matter how frustrated, impatient, or angry you may be, always ensure that your last words to someone you love are words you can live with—because you never know when the line will be cut. We hope that by fostering better, more secure ways for people to stay connected, we might prevent even one person from experiencing the silence that follows a sudden, unresolved goodbye.  
To my mother: This is for you. May your name be a source of safety and connection for all who use this network.
